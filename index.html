About Me

SAP Developer

I am a Software Developer with 5+ years of professional experience in SAP development. At Central Michigan University, I worked as an SAP ABAP Developer, leading ABAP development alongside functional and technical consulting, as well as configuration throughout the full lifecycle of FICO, HCM, and SLCM modules in ECC.

Prior to that, I was part of SAP projects at Infosys, where I extensively worked with ABAP, RAP, and UI5/Fiori on ECC, S/4HANA, and BTP platforms. My experience spans various modules including FICO, SD, and MM, covering the entire development lifecycle—design, build, performance tuning, system integration testing (SIT), UAT, and deployment to quality and production environments.

I hold a Master’s degree in Information Systems with a specialization in SAP from Central Michigan University. I was honored with the Dow Award for SAP Excellence by Dow Chemical Company in April 2025 for successfully developing the comprehensive SAP HCM system, showcasing my technical expertise.

Beyond work, I love traveling, reading novels, and listening to music.
